ADSR

A simple ADSR-type envelope generator.

Inputs:
  • vel: velocity (typically connected from a MIDI velocity output)
  • trg: trigger (typically connected from a MIDI trigger output)
  • sen: velocity sensitivity factor (0=no touch sensitivity, 1=full sensitivity)
  • atk: attack rate (addition per sample)
  • dcy: decay rate (multiplication per sample)
  • sus: sustain level (0=no sustain, 1=sustain at full level)
  • rel: release rate (multiplication per sample)
Outputs:
  • OUT: the output (typically in the range of 0-1)
