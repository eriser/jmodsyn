Karplus-Strong synthesis is a simple form of physical modeling that is useful for simulating plucked strings and such.
A short burst of noise is created (to simulate the 'pluck'), which will resonate in a comb-filter with a strong feedback.

Inputs:
  • frq: the frequency
  • trg: the trigger
  • fb: the feedback
  • cut: filter cut-off frequency (in Hz). Note that much filtering will lead to phase shifts, leading to an incorrect pitch.
  • plu: the time in seconds of the 'pluck'
  • env: the envelope of the pluck
Outputs:
  • OUT: the output
